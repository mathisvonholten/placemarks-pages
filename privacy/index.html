Privacy Policy for Placemarks

Placemarks does not collect, sell, or share personal data from the app.

App data

The app does not use accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking, or a developer server.

Your saved app content and preferences are stored on your device. This includes saved pins, favorites, visited status, notes, settings, and recent searches. Placemarks does not receive or access this data.

Depending on your device settings, Apple may include app data in device backups. Placemarks does not control or access those backups.

Apple Maps

Placemarks uses Apple Maps and MapKit to search for and display places. Search and map requests are handled by Apple according to Apple’s privacy practices.

Support

If you contact support, the information you choose to include in your message is used only to respond to your request. Support messages may include your email address, device details, iOS version, and anything else you choose to send.

Support email is handled through Apple’s iCloud Mail service. Support messages are kept only as long as needed to respond and maintain basic support records, then deleted when no longer needed.

Your choices

You can delete app data by deleting saved pins in the app or by deleting the app from your device. You can request deletion of support messages by contacting support.

You may also contact support to ask about access, correction, or deletion of personal data you have provided in a support message.
