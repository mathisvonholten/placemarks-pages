Privacy Policy for Placemarks

Placemarks does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking, or a developer server.

Places, favorites, visited status, notes, settings, and recent searches are stored on your device.

Placemarks uses Apple Maps and MapKit to search for and display places. Search and map requests are handled by Apple according to Apple’s privacy practices.

If you contact support, any information you choose to include is used only to respond to your request.

Contact: placemarks@icloud.com
